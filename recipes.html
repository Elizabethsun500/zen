Easy Pumpkin Soup

Ingredient list

2 Tablespoons olive oil
1 Medium onion chopped
2 Tablespoons garlic chopped
2 Cups chicken or vegetable soup
2 Cups pumpkin canned
13.5 ounce container coconut milk (not light)
Dash of salt to taste

Directions

Saute onions and garlic in olive oil in large pot over medium heat until onions are soft and fragrant. Add pumpkin, coconut milk and chick or vegetable broth. Simmer 5- 10 minutes. Stir or blend with an emulsifier . Salt to taste. Top with a teaspoon of Twenty-4 Zen Mild Chili Mango Granola.
